Non-surgical intimate rejuvenation treatment designed to support vaginal laxity, confidence, postnatal recovery and menopause-related changes.

What Is Vaginal HIFU?

Vaginal HIFU (High Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-surgical treatment designed to stimulate collagen production and support intimate wellness.

The treatment may help improve symptoms associated with:

  • Vaginal laxity
  • Postnatal pelvic changes
  • Menopause-related vaginal changes
  • Mild urinary leakage
  • Reduced intimate confidence

Benefits of Vaginal HIFU

  • Non-surgical treatment
  • No downtime required
  • Supports collagen stimulation
  • Quick and discreet procedure
  • Designed to improve intimate wellness
  • May support postnatal recovery

Who Is Vaginal HIFU Suitable For?

Vaginal HIFU may be suitable for women experiencing changes following:

  • Childbirth
  • Menopause
  • Age-related collagen loss
  • Changes in intimate confidence

A consultation is recommended to determine whether treatment is appropriate for you.

What To Expect During Treatment

Vaginal HIFU treatment is typically completed within 30–45 minutes. The procedure is non-surgical with minimal downtime, allowing most patients to return to normal daily activities shortly afterwards.
