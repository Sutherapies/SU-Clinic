Women’s Health Physiotherapy in Manchester: What We Treat and How We Help

Women’s health concerns can affect comfort, confidence, and quality of life at every stage. At SU Women’s Health Physiotherapy and Wellness Clinic, we provide specialist women’s health physiotherapy in Manchester city centre, supporting women with pelvic health conditions, pregnancy-related concerns, postnatal recovery, and long-term pelvic floor dysfunction.

What Is Women’s Health Physiotherapy?

Women’s health physiotherapy focuses on the assessment and treatment of the pelvic floor, core, and musculoskeletal system. It can help women experiencing pelvic pain, bladder or bowel dysfunction, prolapse symptoms, pregnancy-related pain, or ongoing issues following childbirth or menopause.

Conditions We Treat

  • Pelvic floor dysfunction
  • Urinary or bowel incontinence
  • Pelvic organ prolapse
  • Pelvic pain and discomfort
  • Pregnancy-related back or pelvic pain
  • Postnatal recovery and rehabilitation
  • Core weakness and diastasis recti
  • Pain with intercourse

Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy in Manchester

Pelvic floor physiotherapy aims to improve muscle strength, coordination, and control, supporting bladder, bowel, and pelvic organ function. Individualised treatment plans are designed to restore confidence and comfort.

Pregnancy and Postnatal Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy during pregnancy can help manage pelvic girdle pain and back pain, while postnatal physiotherapy supports recovery following vaginal or caesarean birth and guides a safe return to exercise.

A Holistic and Individualised Approach

We take a holistic approach to women’s health, considering physical, hormonal, and lifestyle factors. Where appropriate, physiotherapy may be combined with advanced intimate wellness treatments to support overall pelvic wellbeing.
