Intimate Aesthetic Treatments in Manchester

Clinician-Led Intimate Rejuvenation & Vaginal Health Care

At SU Women’s Health Physiotherapy & Wellness we provide safe, discreet and medically-led intimate aesthetic treatments for women experiencing discomfort, dryness, irritation or changes related to ageing, childbirth and menopause.

Many women notice intimate symptoms but are unsure where to seek help. As pelvic health specialists, we understand both the medical and functional causes of these concerns and offer non-surgical treatments designed to restore comfort, hydration and confidence.

All consultations are private, respectful and pressure-free.

What symptoms can intimate aesthetics help with?

  • Vaginal dryness
  • Irritation or burning sensation
  • Discomfort during intercourse
  • Painful intercourse related to dryness
  • Menopause-related intimate symptoms
  • Changes following childbirth
  • Loss of tissue hydration
  • Reduced elasticity in the vulval area

These symptoms are extremely common during perimenopause and menopause, and many women do not realise effective non-surgical treatment options are available.

Our Medical Approach

Unlike cosmetic clinics, treatments are delivered by a women’s health clinician with specialist knowledge of pelvic anatomy, hormones and tissue health.

  • Full medical and symptom history
  • Menopause discussion and screening
  • Treatment suitability assessment
  • Clear explanation of outcomes
  • Aftercare guidance

If treatment is not appropriate, we may recommend pelvic health physiotherapy or GP review. Patient safety always comes first.

Intimate Rejuvenation Treatment

We offer hyaluronic acid-based intimate skin treatments designed to improve hydration, tissue quality and comfort.

  • Improves hydration of vulval tissues
  • Supports tissue elasticity
  • Reduces friction and irritation
  • Improves daily comfort
  • Helps painful intercourse caused by dryness

This is a non-surgical treatment with minimal downtime and can be suitable for women unable to use hormonal therapies.

Menopause & Vaginal Dryness

During menopause, oestrogen levels fall which affects vulval and vaginal tissues. This may cause dryness, irritation and discomfort, sometimes referred to as Genitourinary Syndrome of Menopause (GSM).

Intimate aesthetic treatments can support tissue hydration and comfort alongside pelvic health advice and lifestyle guidance.
